E2SDS

International Colloquium on Energy-Efficient and Sustainable Distributed Systems

November 26-27, 2024  |  Hybrid event: Natal, Brazil & Online

About

The International Colloquium on Energy-Efficient and Sustainable Distributed Systems (E2SDS) seeks to bring together researchers and practitioners to present and discuss the latest research, exchange ideas and experiences, and explore innovative approaches for designing, developing, and deploying energy-efficient and sustainable distributed systems. E2SDS serves as a scientific forum to facilitate discussions on current and future advancements in distributed systems that consider and proactively minimize energy consumption while identifying effective strategies to reduce such consumption and achieve a sufficient and reasonable level of digital use.

Venue

E2SDS will be a hybrid event, taking place in person at Auditorium B205 in the main building of the Digital Metropolis Institute (IMD) - Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte (UFRN), in Natal, Brazil, and live online via Zoom. Registration to E2SDS is free but mandatory. Only registered participants will receive the link for joining online.
